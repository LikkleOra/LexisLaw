# Mokoena Legal Services Digital Platform

## Project Overview

**Project Name:** Mokoena Legal Services Digital Platform  
**Type:** Legal Services Web Application  
**Location:** Johannesburg, South Africa  
**Tech Stack:** Vanilla HTML/CSS/JS (Phase 1) → Full-stack extension planned

## Business Context

Mokoena Legal Services is an established law firm seeking to modernize client interactions through a digital platform. The current implementation provides:
- Landing page with brutalist design aesthetic
- Online booking system for consultations  
- Matter tracking portal for clients

## Goals

1. **Client Acquisition:** Streamline new client intake through online booking
2. **Client Retention:** Provide matter tracking and WhatsApp communication
3. **Operational Efficiency:** Reduce phone/email inquiries through self-service
4. **Brand Positioning:** Differentiate through modern, bold digital presence

## Constraints

- Must maintain POPIA compliance for personal data
- Must integrate with existing WhatsApp Business API
- Design must remain accessible and professional
- Mobile-first approach required
